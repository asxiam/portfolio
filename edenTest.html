This new identity is inspired by the centuries old craft of stone carving–the EDEN animation speaks to the brand’s commitment to quality and depth in their creations. Intricate letterforms are chiseled out of large blocks of marble as molten silver seeps through the cracks—the embodiment of classic technique and modern production.
